Lyca Production

Lyca Productions is an Indian entertainment company, which was established by Subaskaran Allirajah in 2014. A subgroup of Lycamobile, the production studio has been involved in the production and distribution of films made in South India. The Vision of Lyca is to create, promote and release content across varying genres.
